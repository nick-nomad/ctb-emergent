Insulation and Comfort: Designed for 365-Day Use

A timber garden room or office is designed to offer a comfortable, usable environment throughout the year. We ensure maximum value and energy efficiency:

  • Full Insulation Packages: We offer comprehensive floor, wall, and roof insulation for true year-round usability and thermal efficiency.
  • Double Glazing: Standard in our year-round rooms to maintain warmth and reduce noise.
  • Electrics & Heating: Options for full electrical systems, lighting, and ventilation/underfloor heating to guarantee comfort.

Superior Timber Quality and Construction

We focus on delivering high-quality timber buildings at sensible prices. All structural elements are built using premium, slow-grown First-Grade Bulgarian Spruce, ensuring superior strength and durability compared to standard flat-pack alternatives. Our expertise ensures a perfect, weather-resistant finish that lasts decades.

Materials and Styles for Full Customisation

Our garden rooms are supplied in first-grade Bulgarian spruce as standard, with options including softwoods, hardwoods, composite cladding, and metal systems. You can choose from contemporary or traditional styles, or request a custom architectural design.

Standard features include insulation, electricity, and lighting. We can also provide plumbing, underfloor heating, extended glazing, unique roof systems, and mixed-use layouts (e.g., office-and-storage combinations). Visit our photo gallery for inspiration.

Fast, Full-Service Installation and UK Delivery

Buildings are assembled from modular sections prepared in advance, meaning installation is efficient and minimally disruptive.

If you choose our full-service package, we manage the entire project, including advising on or laying the necessary foundations (concrete slabs or pads), right through to the final on-site assembly and connection of services.

Frequently Asked Questions About Our Garden Rooms

Can I use my garden room in winter?

Yes. Our garden rooms are designed to be comfortable throughout the year, with insulation, double glazing and optional heating features.

Can I customise my building?

Yes. Every building can be tailored, including size, layout, materials, insulation, roofing and finishes.

What materials are available?

We use first-grade Bulgarian spruce as standard, with the choice of metal and composite systems depending on design.

Do you deliver and install?

Yes. Buildings are prepared in modular sections for efficient delivery and on-site installation.

What foundations are required for a garden room?

We strongly recommend a reinforced, level concrete slab or concrete pad for a heavy-duty structure like a garden room. We can advise on specifications or offer groundwork services as part of our full installation package.
